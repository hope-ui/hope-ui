# `MESSAGES_IT` — Italian catalog

Built-in **Italian** catalog, selected when the active locale's primary subtag is `it`. Shape + key
list: `../messages.md`. Selection + fallback: `catalogs.md`.

- `datesSelected` plural: singular only at `1`, with feminine agreement on the noun and participle
  (`1 data selezionata` / `2 date selezionate`).
